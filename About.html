Pet Adoption Checklist

Get ready to Adopt Today!

  • Choose the Right Pet

    Consider the pet's size, breed, energy level, and compatibility with your lifestyle.

  • Prepare Your Home

    Pet-proof your space and set up a designated area with a bed, toys, and food/water bowls.

  • Gather Supplies

    Get essentials: collar, leash, food, toys, and, for cats, a litter box.

  • Budget for Ongoing Costs

    Plan for adoption fees, food, vet visits, and potential emergencies.

  • Schedule Vet Care

    Ensure your pet has a vet check-up, vaccinations, and consider microchipping.
